System Status

As of 2009-12-12:

Librelist is currently working, there are no planned outages any time soon.

Problem Sending/Receiving?

If you are sending emails and not seeing a response, there could be two possible causes:

  1. You are using google to host a domain that is not google.com. Don’t do this. Send email from a real domain and server that matches.
  2. You may have bounced and have been disabled. Try sending an email to unbounce@librelist.com to reset your state.

If it’s neither of these, then please put in a support ticket and indicate your email address so we can reset your account.
